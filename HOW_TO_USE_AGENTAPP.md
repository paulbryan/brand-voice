## üîß Troubleshooting

### "AgentApp not found"
- Double-check the ID: `TMXhceRjWM`
- Make sure you're in a CREAO workspace
- Try refreshing the page

### "Access denied"
- The AgentApp might be private to your account
- Make sure you're logged into the correct CREAO account

### "Error loading form"
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache
- Contact CREAO support

### "WebFetch failed" (during Phase 1)
- The URL might be blocked or inaccessible
- Try pasting the text directly instead
- Check if the URL requires authentication

## üéØ Pro Tips

### For Best Voice Analysis
- Use 5 samples (not just 3) for more accurate results
- Choose your BEST content (viral posts, well-received articles)

- Be specific with profile names (e.g., "LinkedIn Founder Voice" not just "My Voice")
